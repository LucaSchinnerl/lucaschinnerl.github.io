Data science

Projects completed in data science and data analysis

  • Learn More

My interested in data science began in my final year of my bachelors degree. Through the Erasmus+ scholarshop I had the opportunity to visit the Catholic University of Leuven. Here I was introduced to Monte Carlo Simulations and its applications to statistical physics. Additionally, I studied applications of machine learning to solve equations in quantum many body problems. I deeped my knowledge in the application of artificall intellgence in the insurance sector while working for KPMG.

I am currently studying as a graduate student in physics. Data science is a major part of my research. I have explored applications of machine learning in fundamental physics. Through the LMUexchange scholarship I studied data science and analytics in South Korea, with a focus on deep learning. Finally, I am currently working on my thesis at the Belle II experiment, a particle physics experiment designed to study the properties of B mesons. Here I employ machine learning techniques to find ultra rare events.
